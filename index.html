About Us CURRENT is an all natural cosmetic company with products that are carefully constructed to benefit your body the most without harming the natural environment.

There is an enormous amount of plastic waste created by the cosmetic industry. Most products also contain harmful chemicals that are infiltrating our bodies and our water systems. The products most people put on their body on a daily basis contain harmful chemicals that can alter DNA, change hormone levels, and cause cancer. These products are absorbed through your skin and go directly into your bloodstream. The products also wash down the drain and go directly into our water systems. Tons of sunscreen ends up in the ocean and chemicals such as oxybenzone are detrimental to coral reefs and fisheries.

CURRENT is out here to change the industry. We are creating products that are good for your body and the planet.

We use the highest quality NATURAL and PLANT BASED ingredients.
